  • Markdown is basically headings, code, and bullets (nested). You really don't even need tables since bullets do the job quite well. With Doxygen, yoiu get everyting linked together as a unit, thus no wandering around to other documents in other areas.

Remember these rules in documentation:

  1. Single source.
  2. Link everything together so users can get where they want to go with the 3-click rule.
  3. Don't pay for clunky, buggy authoring tools since the future is markdown and GitHub.
