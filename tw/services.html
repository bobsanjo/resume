The Teknowrite Quiz

Take this quiz to see how your documentation stands up.

• Are there typos/grammar mistakes on the first pages of text?
• Is the Table of Contents incorrect (hyperlinks don't work) or missing?
• Is the Index missing? Technical documents typically have more than one index.
• Are key terms spelled differently (e.g., settop box or set-top box or SetTop box)?
• Are the documents poorly structured and hard to navigate (no hyperlinks)?
• Are the graphics unclear, unlabled, or mislabeled?
• Does the formatting look as if someone had a "font fest" that would make Johann Gutenberg blush? FYI-- a font is the typeface and its size (e.g., Arial - 9pt), not simply the typeface (e.g., Arial) as most people commonly describe the term.

Answering Yes to two or more of these questions indicates your documenation needs work-- probably more than you realize. If you notice documentation problems, you can be sure your customers and users will notice and judge your product or service appropriately.
