We specialize in creating long-term partnerships with our clients helping companies such as Cisco, Kohler and ITT deliver documentation and courseware that go beyond words on a page or computer screen to create a positive customer experience. The InfoPros team of employees has the knowledge, experience and resources to handle most any need. Our client-focused approach and national presence allow InfoPros to provide a wide array of options from individual on-site support to complete outsourcing solutions. Clients feel comfortable knowing that InfoPros is the safe choice for companies wanting a partner to support their documentation and courseware development needs.
