About this Site

This site demonstrates the 1-click rule to get to information. For example:

Mouse over ASSY INSTRUCTIONS > Mr. Potato Head > Carribean Pirate

Click the PDF to see the PDF file.

Click markdown to see the markdown formatted version of the PDF file.

Click the Video to see the video that goes with the PDF.

It's all done with HTML .css and javascript. Nothing fancy or third-party. Just some creativity.

Plus it reduces all the clutter. MIT professor Patrick Winston has a great lecture on this here (31:50, the Too Heavy Crime as he calls it) if you're intrested in how to deliver information instead of clutter.
